Guy Grossi may be famed for his Italian cuisine, but the chef’s dishes were (until now) only to be found in Melbourne. That’s all set to change thanks to a new collaboration series called ‘Luke & Friends’ at glass brasserie in the Hilton Sydney. The partnership between glass’ Luke Mangan and Guy will see a series of jointly crafted dishes launch until the end of 2017.
